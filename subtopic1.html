Abstract
 This website here showcases the entire timeline of machine learning, from its early conception to the current day. It includes the year an event occurred and a brief description of what that event was. The timeline here begins in 1943 with Logician Walter Pitts and neuroscientist Warren McCulloch publishing the first mathematical modeling for a neural network. The timeline ends in 2023 with OpenAi announcing GPT-4 multimodal LLM. After the timeline ends, the site discusses the future of machine learning and its advancements in different fields. Multiple images are present on the website that show the evolution of chatbots and the difference between CNNs and GANs, two types of Neural Networks.
