The Timeline Machine Learning
Site Author
 The EasyDOS Tech Forum


Abstract
 Site contains a basic timeline of the beginnings of computers in education until the present.  Includes years and a breif description of world history along with the important advances in computer technology.  Starts with the year 1946 and the invention of the first vacuum tube computer used in the war effort to 1958 and the introduction of technology in the schools,  to 25% of classrooms in 1986 utilizing computers all the way until 1994 when most schools had computers in the classroom.  Also describes the evolution of the types of computers being used in businesses, schools, spacestations, cold war efforts, etc.

 Additional Timeline of Machine Learning
Site Author
 Mary Bellis
Abstract
 This site also is a timeline of the evolution of computers.  It includes information from 1936 up until 1985 with the creation of Microsoft Windows and the war with Apple.  This site differs from the first one because each year is actually a link to an in depth article about the particular advancement in computing during that year.  For example, unlike the first site, it provides a linke for the year 1946 to a detailed article about the dicovery and invention of the first vacuum tube computer.  It goes into detail about the way it was made, the price and the tasks it performed, and when it was "retired".  It is set up in a table- like fashion and next to the year it gives the type of computer that was invented and also their corresponding inventors.  It then gives a very brief description of the event.
